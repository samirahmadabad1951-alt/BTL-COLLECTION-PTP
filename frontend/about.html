About EcoStore

Built for a planet we want to keep.

EcoStore is the trusted marketplace for verified sustainable goods. We believe shopping should leave the world better than we found it — so we audit every seller, score every product, and plant a tree with every order.

Our Mission

To accelerate the transition to a sustainable economy by making eco-friendly products accessible, transparent, and trustworthy.

Verified Eco

Every seller and product is audited by our sustainability team.

Plant-Positive

We plant a tree for every order shipped.

Climate Honest

We measure CO₂ saved per item, transparently.

Maker First

Fair margins for small, eco-conscious makers.
