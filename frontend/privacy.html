Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026

We collect only what we need to deliver your orders and keep your account secure: name, email, shipping address, and order history.

What we don't do

We never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for unrelated marketing.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to remember your cart, language, and theme preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also request a copy of your data export or complete account deletion.

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request permanent account deletion
  • Export: Download your data in JSON format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@ecostore.app. We respond within 30 days.

Data security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Payment information is processed by secure third-party providers — we never store full card details.

Third-party services

We use trusted partners for payment processing (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Stripe), shipping (DHL, Aramex), and analytics (Plausible, privacy-friendly). Each partner complies with applicable data protection laws.

Children's privacy

EcoStore is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be notified via email or website banner. Continued use of EcoStore constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For privacy questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@ecostore.app.
